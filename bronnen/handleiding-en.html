1. Getting started

MyFamTreeCollab runs entirely in your browser. No installation needed — just open the website and start. Your data is stored locally on your device by default.

Demo mode: If you open the app without an account, load the demo family tree — it contains two families (De Vries and Martens) across multiple generations. You can try out all features this way; demo data is not saved. Create a free account to build and save your own family tree.

The navigation bar at the top takes you to all sections: Manage, Family Tree, Timeline, Statistics and Export.

2. Creating an account and logging in

An account gives you access to local storage and cloud synchronisation across devices. Registration is free. There are two account types:

  • Basic account guest — free. Local family tree up to 60 people. No cloud, no collaboration. All new accounts start as basic accounts.
  • Owner account owner — via a donation (~€5 per 6 months). Unlimited people, cloud backup, sharing family trees and inviting others.
💡 Viewer and Editor are not account types. They are family tree permissions that an Owner grants for a specific tree. See section 14 for more details.

Creating an account

  • Click Login in the bar at the top of the page.
  • Click the "Create account" tab.
  • Enter your username, email address and password.
  • Click "Create account".
  • You will receive a confirmation email — click the link to activate your account.
  • You can then log in. Your account starts as a Basic account.

Logging in

  • Click Login in the bar at the top.
  • Enter your email address and password.
  • Click "Log in".
  • Your username will appear at the top of the page.

Logging out

Click your username at the top to open the user menu, then click "🚪 Log out".

User menu

  • ⚙️ My Account — profile, password, cloud family trees
  • 📜 Version history — view and restore earlier versions
  • 💬 Collaborate — message board. Red number = unread messages.
  • 🚪 Log out — ends your session and clears local data

Notification badge

A red number appears next to your username when new messages are posted in family trees you have access to. It disappears automatically when you visit the Collaborate page.

3. Adding your first person

Every family tree starts with one person — the Main ID. This is usually yourself or the person whose family tree you are building.

  • Go to Start Now on the homepage, or click Manage in the navigation.
  • Click "New family tree" if you have no people yet.
  • Fill in the details: First name and Last name are required, the rest is optional.
  • Click "Save".
  • Check the details in the preview panel.
  • Click "Confirm & go to Manage".
Tip: You can always edit details later via the Manage page.

4. Managing your family tree

On the Manage page you add family members, edit existing people and record relationships.

Adding a person

  • Go to Manage in the navigation.
  • Click "+ Add".
  • Fill in the details. FatherID, MotherID and PartnerID link the person to others.
  • Click "Save".

Editing a person

  • Search for the person using the search bar at the top.
  • The table shows the selected person with their family.
  • Edit the details directly and click "Save".

Deleting a person

  • Search for the person using the search bar.
  • Click the 🗑️ icon on the left of the row.
  • Confirm the deletion. The ID is automatically removed from all relationships.

Recording relationships

FatherID, MotherID and PartnerID support multiple values. Each cell shows a dynamic list of input fields.

  • FatherID — one or more fathers (e.g. adoption, stepfather)
  • MotherID — one or more mothers
  • PartnerID — one or more partners

Adding and removing IDs

  • Click "+ ID" below a field to add an extra input.
  • Click "×" next to an input to remove that ID.
  • The last field is cleared rather than removed.
  • Click "Save" to store your changes.
Storage: Values are stored internally as P-001|P-003 — the widget handles this automatically.

6. Viewing your family tree

The Family Tree page shows a visual overview of family members around a selected person.

  • Click Family Tree in the navigation.
  • Search for a person using the search bar.
  • The tree shows the selected person (yellow) with parents (green), partner(s) (purple) and children (blue).
  • Click a node to make that person the new centre.

7. Timeline

The Timeline shows all family members on a horizontal time axis. Each person is a bar from birth year to death year.

  • Click Timeline in the navigation.
  • Search for a person using the search bar.
  • The timeline loads automatically with all family members grouped by generation.
  • Hover over a bar for more details.

8. Statistics

The Statistics page gives an overview of your family tree: number of people, gender distribution, oldest and youngest person.

  • Click Statistics in the navigation.

9. My Account

The account page gives you a complete overview of your profile and settings. Accessible via the user menu at the top right.

Profile

At the top you see your avatar, username, email address, account type and membership date.

Local data & active family tree

  • The choice is saved immediately to your account.

Changing your password

  • Confirm the password in the second field.

10. Version history

Version history lets you view, compare and restore earlier versions of a family tree. A maximum of 5 versions are kept per tree.

Required: Logged in as Owner.
  • Go to My Account → 📜 Version history.
  • Choose a family tree from the dropdown.
  • Click "👁 Preview" to view a version without loading it.
  • Click "⏪ Restore" to revert to a version.
  • Click "🔍 Compare" twice to view two versions side by side.
↑ Back to top

11. Cloud family trees

With an Owner account you can save multiple family trees to the cloud.

Required: Owner to save. Viewers/Editors can load shared trees via "🔗 Shared with me".

Saving a family tree

  • Go to Storage → ☁️ Cloud family trees.
  • Enter a name and click "➕ Save as new".

Loading a family tree

  • Go to Storage → ☁️ Cloud family trees.
  • Click "⬇️ Load" next to the desired tree.
  • The tree is now available locally and marked as ACTIVE.

Deleting a family tree

  • Click "🗑️ Delete" next to the tree.
  • Confirm the prompt. The ACTIVE bar disappears automatically.

12. Importing

You can import existing family tree data via a CSV file. During import, duplicate IDs are detected automatically.

  • Download the CSV template.
  • Fill in the template with your family data.
  • Go to Import via the navigation.
  • Click "Choose file" and select your CSV.
  • Click "Import".

Handling duplicates

If an imported ID already exists in your family tree, a dialog appears showing the number of duplicates and the affected IDs. Choose how to proceed:

  • ⏭ Skip — import only new people, skip duplicates
  • ♻️ Overwrite — replace existing people with the imported version
  • 📥 Import all anyway — add everything including duplicates
  • ✖ Cancel — stop the import, nothing is saved
Note: Only available for Owner accounts.

13. Exporting

Export your family tree as CSV or JSON. Available for all logged-in accounts (Basic and Owner).

15. Sharing & collaborating

As an Owner you can invite others to view or contribute to your family tree. Invited users receive a tree permission: Viewer or Editor.

Required: Logged in as Owner. The tree must be saved to the cloud.

Inviting someone

  • Go to Storage → ☁️ Cloud family trees.
  • Click "🔗 Share" next to the tree.
  • Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.
  • Choose a permission: 👁 Viewer or ✏️ Editor.
  • Click "Invite".
Note: The invited user must already have an account. A free basic account is sufficient.

Revoking access

  • Open the share panel via "🔗 Share".
  • Click "Revoke" next to the user.

Viewing shared trees (Viewer/Editor)

  • Go to Storage → 🔗 Shared with me.
  • Click "⬇️ Load" to load the tree.

16. Message board (Collaboration)

The message board shows all family trees you have access to. Per tree you see discussions about specific people, organised by person ID.

Required: Logged in with access to at least one family tree.

Opening the message board

  • Click 💬 Collaborate in the navigation or in the user menu.
  • All trees you have access to are loaded, each in its own section.

Active vs. read-only trees

  • ✓ Active (blue accent line) — the loaded tree. You can start discussions and post messages here.
  • Read only — other trees. Click the link in the yellow banner to activate that tree.

Starting a discussion

  • Type a name or ID in the search bar of the active tree.
  • Click the desired person in the dropdown.
  • Confirm in the modal — a new discussion block appears.

Posting a message

  • Open a discussion block by clicking its header.
  • Type your message in the text field at the bottom.
  • Click "Send".

Discussion status

StatusMeaning
🆕 NewNot yet picked up
🔓 OpenActive and pending
🔄 In progressSomeone is working on it
✅ ClosedResolved

Posting a change proposal (Editor/Owner)

  • Click "📝 Propose change" in the message form.
  • Describe the proposed change in the yellow text field.
  • Send the message — the proposal appears as a separate yellow block.

Deleting a message

Owners can delete any message via ✕. Viewers and Editors can only delete their own messages.
