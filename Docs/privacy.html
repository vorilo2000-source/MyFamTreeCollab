Privacy Policy

MyFamTreeCollab respects the privacy of visitors and users of this website.

What data is collected

This website may contain genealogical data such as names, dates of birth and family relationships.

Living persons

Data relating to living persons is displayed as limitedly as possible to protect their privacy.

Purpose of the data

The data is used solely for genealogical research and the visualisation of family relationships.

External services

The website may make use of external hosting services such as GitHub Pages. These services may collect technical data such as IP addresses or log files.

Correction or deletion

If you believe that certain information is incorrect or should be removed, please contact the administrator of this website.
